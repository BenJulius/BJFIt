# BJ Fit Play Store Readiness

## Live policy URLs required
- Privacy Policy: `https://bjfit.benjulius.dev/privacy`
- Terms of Service: `https://bjfit.benjulius.dev/terms`
- Account/Data Deletion: `https://bjfit.benjulius.dev/data-deletion`

## Before submission
1. Ensure domain `bjfit.benjulius.dev` is live over HTTPS.
2. In Supabase Auth settings, set Site URL and redirect URLs to BJ Fit domain.
3. In Google Play Console, complete Data safety form using actual app behavior.
4. Provide support email and privacy contact email in Store listing.
5. Upload app icon (512x512) and feature graphic (1024x500).

## App declarations to verify
- Account creation/login methods.
- Data types collected (email, workout data, usage analytics if enabled).
- Account deletion availability in app (Profile > Delete Account).
- Ads and in-app purchase disclosures (if enabled).

## Release quality checks
- Test fresh install and login.
- Test logout/login persistence.
- Test account deletion flow.
- Test legal links from onboarding screen.
